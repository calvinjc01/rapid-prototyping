Our current design has light focused down through the center of the reflector and bounced off of a mirror onto the bottom of the salt battery. First, I performed trigonometric calculations to determine what kind of heights and distances would result in the light hitting the reflector when the sun is at an elevation angle of 20-30 degrees, the range of peak sunlight hours in Boston in late November.
