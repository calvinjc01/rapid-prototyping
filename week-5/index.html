First, the idea for an integrated system. My goal here was to design a solar collection method that didn't require solar tracking. The result was a roof with a flat top and four angled sides made out of fresnel lenses. That way, regardless of the position of the sun or the structure, sunlight would be focused. The first iteration of this involved a long tube of reflective material that would direct the light down and onto a collection surface. The benefit of the tube would be a collection surface much closer to the other systems, minimizing thermodynamic losses.
