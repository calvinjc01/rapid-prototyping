The overarching concept is to find a way to harness energy from the environment. The ideas ranged from didactic sculptures, to cooking tools, to electric bike motors. My group discussed several different ideas: * Using solar and/or wind to charge an electric bike motor * Generating electricity with a revolving door * Heating sidewalks with waste heat from the sewer * A display with two rubber surfaces- one with an enclosure of air above and one with an enclosure of CO2- to demonstrate the difference in heat from greenhouse gases * Focusing sunlight to grill food in a pavilion, utilising a wind tower to cool the pavilion (working title: the grillin' pavillion) We also did some preliminary research on wind towers: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windcatcher ![wind tower](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windcatcher#/media/File:Malqaf.svg)
